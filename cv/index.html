Publications

  • 2025.09
    Q-Guided Flow Q-Learning
    CoRL 2025 Workshop RemembeRL
    Authors: Yejun Jang, Hong Chul Nam, Jeong Min Park, Gimin Bae, Hyun Kwon. An RL training framework mathematically equivalent to the recently popularized Flow Q-Learning, but with decoupled actor and critic training for improved computational efficiency and quicker hyperparameter tuning.
  • 2025.02
    Exploring the Structure and Hyperparameters of a DQN Model for Penetration Testing on Large-Scale Networks
    Symposium of the Korean Institute of Communications and Information Sciences (KICS)
    Authors: Yejun Jang, Jihun Shin, Janghyong Lee. Investigation of Deep Q-Network architectures and hyperparameter configurations for automated penetration testing.
  • 2024.11
    Preventing Model and Data Poisoning Attacks on Military AI Systems via Supply Chain Verification Techniques
    Conference on Information Security and Cryptography Winter 2024 (CISC-W)
    Authors: Yejun Jang, Rocky Kim, Yeongwha Lee. Research on securing military AI systems against adversarial attacks through supply chain verification.
  • 2023.11
    Living Ideas from Future Observers (미래 관찰자의 살아있는 아이디어)
    Porche Books
    Book authored by the Institute for Future Strategy of Seoul National University. Participated as an editor and contributor.
  • 2020.05
    A Study on Real-Time Feedback System for Forward Head Posture Using Markerless Skeletal Tracking
    International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF) - Finalist
    Authors: Yejun Jang, Sunjin Kim. Developed a contactless feedback system using computer vision to detect and correct Forward Head Posture, achieving 81% improvement.
