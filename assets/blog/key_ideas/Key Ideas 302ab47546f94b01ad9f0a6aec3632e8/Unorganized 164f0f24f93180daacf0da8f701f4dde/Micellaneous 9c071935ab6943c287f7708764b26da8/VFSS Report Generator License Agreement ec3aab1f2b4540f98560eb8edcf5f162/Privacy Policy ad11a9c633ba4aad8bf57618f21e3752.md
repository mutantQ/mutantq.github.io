# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: Oct 17, 2023**

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") delineates the practices of VFSS Report Generator ("we", "our", or "us") concerning the collection, use, and protection of personal information provided by users ("you" or "your") of our application.

**1. Scope and Application**

This Policy applies solely to the information collected when you utilize VFSS Report Generator to generate reports for the Videofluoroscopic Swallowing Study (VFSS).

**2. Data Collection**

In the course of using our application, you may be prompted to furnish certain personally identifiable information, including, but not limited to:

- **Hospital/Institutional Details**
Hospital Name, Address, Telephone Number, Fax Number, Email
- **Test Information**
Test Number, Test Date, Tester/Examiner
- **Patient Information**
Hospital Registration Number
Gender/Age
Patient Name
Department (Medical Specialty)
Onset Date (Date of Disease Onset or Diagnosis)
Clinical Diagnosis
Current Diet
Additional Information/Notes
- **Test Findings, Conclusion and Diagnosis**
- **Report Authorship**

**3. Purpose of Data Utilization**

The personally identifiable information you provide is used exclusively for the purpose of generating a VFSS report in PDF format. We affirm that this information is not employed for any ancillary purposes nor is it disseminated to third-party entities.

**4. Retention of Personal Data**

We wish to assert that the personally identifiable information tendered by you is not retained or archived beyond the requisite duration necessary for generating the report. Upon the conclusion of this process, all such data is expunged from our systems.

**5. Data Security**

The safeguarding of your personal information is of paramount importance to us. We employ rigorous security protocols to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of the data you provide. Notwithstanding, it must be acknowledged that no electronic transmission or storage mechanism is impervious to intrusion. While we endeavor to protect your personal data with commercially viable tools and techniques, we cannot vouchsafe its inviolable security.

**6. Amendments to This Policy**

We reserve the right to revise, amend, or modify this Policy from time to time. Any alterations to this Policy will be duly posted on this page. We encourage users to periodically review this Policy to remain informed of any changes.

**7. Contact and Queries**
